Music Style Transfer with CycleGANs


By

  • Rohin Nanavati
  • David McDevitt
  • Shruthi Hariharan

Description

In a nutshell, our project converts piano audio to guitar and vice versa. We use BigVGAN to convert raw audio waveforms to mel-spectrograms and the generated spectrogram images back to audio. We use CycleGAN to learn the spectrogram-to-spectrogram translatio between the two instruments.
